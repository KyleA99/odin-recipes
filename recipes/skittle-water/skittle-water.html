Skittle Water

Skittle Water - a nutritious beverage for kids and adults

A delicacy from my childhood that is also a superfood

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Your favorite flavor of skittles
  • Granulated sugar

Steps

  1. Fill a 6-12 oz glass with water
  2. Add 10-15 skittles to the glass of water
  3. Add 1-2 tablespoons of granulated sugar to the beverage
  4. Mix with a spoon until the sugar has dissolved
  5. Enjoy
