Peppermint Bark

Image taken from bettycrocker.com

Quick and effortless peppermint bark

A delicious recipe to utilize for holiday christmas bark

Ingredients

  • 1-2 pounds of white chocolate
  • Half a pound of crushed candy canes

Steps

  1. Melt white chocolate in the microwave or a double boiler
  2. Mix in half of your crushed candy canes into the melted white chocolate
  3. Evently spread the melted white chocolate and candy cane mixture into a line cookie sheet
  4. Add the remaining crushed candy canes onto the top of your still-warm bark
  5. Allow your bark to cool in the refrigerator until solid
  6. Enjoy
