Delicious, one-pot spaghetti dish

A simple and cost efficient spaghetti recipe that is sure to satisfy anyone and everyone!

The simplicity of this dish combined with the small list of required ingredients make this a dish perfect for anyone ranging from the sleep-deprived college student to the busy parent.

Ingredients

  • Store-bought spaghetti pasta
  • Your choice of marinara sauce
  • Fresh basil
  • 1 fresh garlic clove
  • Salt
  • Crushed black pepper
  • 5 quarts of water

Steps

  1. Bring approximately 5 quarts of salted water to a boil
  2. Add one package of uncooked pasta to the boiling water and reduce heat to a gentle boil
  3. Intermittently stir pasta and cook for approximately 11 minutes, or until pasta reaches desired texture
  4. Remove pasta from heat and strain out the water
  5. Finely mince garlic and basil and add to pasta sauce
  6. Add pasta sauce to cooked spaghetti
  7. Add salt and pepper to spaghetti until desired flavor is achieved
  8. Enjoy!
